# 6 Steps to Stop Being Shady Online

1. **Audit Your Headers**: Most devs skip HSTS and CSP. Don't be that guy.
2. **Rotate Your Keys**: If your API keys are older than your last haircut, you're at risk.
3. **MFA Everything**: If it doesn't have 2FA, it's not yours.
4. **Scrub Your Metadata**: EXIF data is a treasure map for bad actors.
5. **Monitor Your DNS**: SPF/DMARC are your first line of defense against phishing.
6. **Scan Before They Do**: Use ShadyScanner.com before the hackers do.
